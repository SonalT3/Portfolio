Project Overview

Brief Introduction

Right Route is a student-friendly ride-sharing and multimodal transport solution designed to tackle common commuting challenges faced by students. This project aims to provide a reliable, cost-effective, and time-efficient transportation system by integrating carpooling, live tracking, and public transit options in one app.

Problem Statement

Research, surveys, and student feedback reveal that transportation is a major barrier to academic success.

* Students struggle with high commuting costs, unreliable public transport, and long travel times.

* Existing ride-sharing options (like Uber and Lyft) are expensive for students.

* Apps like Poparide lack live tracking, making carpooling unreliable.

* There is no app that integrates ride-sharing with public transit for flexibility.

Right Route was designed to reduce commuting stress by offering students an affordable and reliable alternative while improving their daily mobility and academic punctuality.

Research & Insights

User Research and Pain Points

We conducted surveys and interviews to understand the challenges students face in transportation.

  • Unreliable Public Transport
  • High Costs
  • Long Travel Times
  • Lack of Live Tracking
  • Safety Concerns
  • Wheather Support

Competitor Analysis

We compared Uber, Lyft, and Poparide and found that:

* Uber & Lyft are costly for students.

*Poparide is cheaper but lacks real-time tracking.

*None of these apps integrate multiple transport modes (carpool + bus).

Based on this research, Right Route was designed to solve these problems.

Wireframes & Design Process

Design Process: Information Architecture, User Flow & Sketches

The design process of Right Route followed a structured UX methodology to ensure an intuitive, user-friendly, and efficient commuting solution for students.

Information Architecture

We started by defining the core functionalities and organizing the app’s content. The goal was to ensure logical navigation and easy access to key features like booking, live tracking, and ride-sharing. The IA helped map out the hierarchy of screens, ensuring an efficient structure for users.

User Flow

After finalizing IA, we created the user flow diagrams to visualize how users would navigate the app. The entry points (e.g., login, home page) and task sequences (e.g., booking a ride, finding a carpool, tracking live rides) were mapped out. This process ensured a seamless experience with minimal friction.

Sketches

These hand-drawn sketches helped visualize layouts, navigation, and key interactions.

Design Logic and Key Takeaway

The design logic behind Right Route is rooted in user needs, usability principles, and efficiency. Every decision in the information architecture, user flow, and wireframes was based on solving core transportation issues for students while ensuring a seamless, intuitive, and safe experience.

Low-Fidelity

Purpose of Low-Fidelity Wireframes: The first digital representation of our sketches. Focuses on layout, structure, and functionality rather than design aesthetics. Used to test early user flows and get initial feedback before moving to detailed designs.

What Low-Fidelity Wireframes Include:

Basic screen layouts for essential features. Placement of navigation, buttons, and key elements. Initial interaction design (e.g., how users book a ride, access tracking, or switch between transport modes).

Key Decisions & Adjustments:

User testing at this stage revealed that the booking flow needed simplification → We revised button placements and form structures. Users wanted a quicker way to see nearby carpool options → We introduced a "Quick Book" feature.

Mid-Fidelity

Purpose of Mid-Fidelity Wireframes:

 The refined version of low-fidelity wireframes with better-defined UI elements. Introduces typography, spacing, and layout consistency without final colors and branding. Helps in testing usability & navigation before proceeding to high-fidelity designs.

Key Features & Decisions in Mid-Fidelity Wireframes:

 Live Tracking Screen – Clearly shows the ride’s real-time location. Booking Flow Improved – Step-by-step guided flow for easy ride selection & confirmation. Safety & Verification Features – User ratings and profile verification added for trust & security. Multimodal Travel Integration – Users can combine carpooling with public transport for flexibility.
