Project Overview

Brief Introduction

Objective: To design an intuitive e-commerce platform that enhances the online shopping experience.

Target Audience: Fashion-forward individuals looking for a hassle-free shopping journey.

Features: Homepage, Category Page, Product Page, Cart, Checkout, and Order Confirmation.

Problem Statement

Research, surveys, and feedbacks to reavel how to run an e-commerce business.

* Shoppers often face frustration with complicated navigation, unclear product descriptions, and lengthy checkout processes on e-commerce platforms.

* The challenge is to design an intuitive, seamless shopping experience where users can easily browse products, view detailed information, add items to the cart, and check out quickly.

* The goal is to eliminate barriers and provide a smooth, enjoyable online shopping journey that encourages conversions and repeat business.

Research & Insights

User Research and Pain Points

Market Study: Analyzed fashion retailers like Zara, ASOS, and H&M for UX patterns.

User Behavior Analysis: Identified challenges in checkout abandonment and product discovery.

Key Findings: Users value fast-loading images, clear product descriptions, and easy navigation.

  • Limited Filters
  • Inaccurate Description
  • Slow Checkout
  • Poor Mobile Experience
  • Shipping Delays
  • Lack of Review

Competitor Analysis

1. Zara

• ✅ High-quality product images and sleek UI. • ✅ Strong focus on minimalist, modern design. • ❌ Checkout process is complicated, leading to drop-offs.

2. ASOS

• ✅ Wide product range with detailed category filters. • ✅ Strong presence in the online fashion space. • ❌ Slow return process, frustrating for customers.

3. H&M

• ✅ Affordable pricing and recognizable brand. • ✅ Offers a mix of trendy and classic fashion. • ❌ Limited product descriptions make size selection difficult.

4. Fashion Nova

• ✅ Trendy, Instagram-friendly designs attract younger audiences. • ✅ Strong social media presence with influencer marketing. • ❌ Shipping delays and inconsistent sizing lead to customer dissatisfaction.
