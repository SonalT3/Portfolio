Project Overview

Brief Introduction

Objective: To create a seamless, user-friendly experience for exchanging currencies online.

Target Audience: Travelers, international students, and businesses needing currency exchange.

Features: Homepage, About, and Contact page with real-time exchange rates, conversion tools, and user-friendly navigation.

Problem Statement

A currency exchange platform designed for seamless international money transfers.

* Users looking to exchange currency online often face high transaction fees, hidden charges, slow processing times, and lack of transparency.

* The existing solutions do not offer a streamlined experience that prioritizes real-time updates, ease of use, and trustworthiness.

* How might we design a currency exchange platform that is transparent, user-friendly, and efficient while ensuring quick transactions and competitive rates?

Research & Insights

User Research and Pain Points

Market Analysis: Studied competitors like Wise, Revolut, and XE.com to identify UX patterns.

User Research: Analyzed common pain points such as high fees, complicated conversions, and lack of trust in exchange platforms.

Key Findings: Users prefer real-time rate updates, transparency in fees, and quick transactions.

  • Unrealiable authorities
  • High Costs
  • Lack of Live Tracking
  • Safety Concerns
  • Lack of Guidance

Competitor Analysis

1. Wise (formerly TransferWise)

• ✅ Low-cost international transfers with transparent fees. • ✅ Uses real exchange rates (mid-market rates). • ❌ Processing time can be slow (1-2 days in some cases).

2. Revolut

• ✅ Offers multi-currency wallets for easy currency management. • ✅ Competitive exchange rates with minimal markup. • ❌ Limited availability in certain countries.

3. XE.com

• ✅ Provides real-time exchange rates and rate alerts. • ✅ Well-known currency conversion tool. • ❌ No direct money transfer services like competitors.

4. PayPal Xoom

• ✅ Fast transfers and secure transactions. • ✅ Well-integrated with PayPal ecosystem. • ❌ High transfer fees and poor exchange rates.
